# Step 1:  Access the App Website

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/intro1.png"
/>

- Open any web browser on your device (e.g., Safari, Chrome, Firefox, or Edge)
- In the search bar, type [RabbitHoles.AI](http://RabbitHoles.AI)
- Press Enter to search

## Step 2: Navigate to the Download Page

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/intro2.png"
/>

- Locate and click on Download for Mac OS (Silicon) or Download for Mac OS (Intel) whichever is compatible with your computer
- Wait for the download to complete - this may take a few minutes depending on your internet connection
- Once the download is finished, locate the downloaded file in your Downloads folder or the location you specified

## Step 3: Download and Install the App


<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/intro3.png"
/>

- Choose the desktop version of the app
- Click on the download button to start the download process
- Once downloaded, locate the installation file in your Downloads folder
- Double-click the file to begin the installation process
- Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation

## Step 4: Open the App


<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/intro4.png"
/>

- After installation, locate the app icon on your desktop or in your Applications folder
- Double-click the icon to launch the app

<img

<img
