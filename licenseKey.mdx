# 🛠 Rabbitholes License Key Activation Guide  

## 📥 Step 1: Download and Install Rabbitholes  
1. Visit the **[official Rabbitholes website](https://rabbitholes.ai)**.  
2. Download the latest version of the application for your operating system (**Windows/macOS/Linux**).  
3. Install the application by following the on-screen instructions.  

## 🚀 Step 2: Open the Application  
1. Launch **Rabbitholes** after installation.  
2. You will see a **License Key Activation** screen. 

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/licesne1.png"
/>

## 🔑 Step 3: Enter Your License Key  
1. Locate your **License Key** (sent via email after the purchase).  
2. Copy and paste the key into the **License Key Input Box** on the activation screen.  

## ✅ Step 4: Activate Your License  
1. Click the **Activate** button.  
2. The system will verify your key:  
   - ✅ **If valid**, you will be redirected to the **dashboard**.  
   - ❌ **If invalid**. Ensure:  
     - The key is entered **correctly** (avoid spaces or typos).  
     - Your **internet connection** is active.  
     - The license **hasn’t expired**.  

## 🎯 Step 5: Start Using Rabbitholes  
1. Once activated, you can **create your first Canvas**.  
2. Start adding **nodes and connecting ideas** 

## 🔍 Troubleshooting & Support  
- If you face activation issues, contact **[support@rabbitholes.ai](mailto:support@rabbitholes.ai)**.  
- For **lost keys**, request a reset from the **[support page](https://rabbitholes.ai/support)**.  

Now you’re ready to **dive into your Rabbitholes! 🚀**
